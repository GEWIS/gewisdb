in package
  • gewisdb
implements FormSelectable

Studies.php : 33

Enum with studies

This enum does not have a notion of 'deprecated' studies because, in contrast to the board functions, it should be a point-in-time representation of the studies that are offered at M&CS and not a historic representation. If a study is no longer offered, it should be removed from this enum, and any members should be migrated to the new program. Removal of a variant should be done only when the study is no longer offered, even to existing students: joining GEWIS is not exclusive to freshmen, so you may encounter students who follow a study that is no longer offered to new students.

"Opleidingscode" in Centraal Register Opleidingen Hoger Onderwijs (CROHO) added. This is also the source of the translation in the language files.
